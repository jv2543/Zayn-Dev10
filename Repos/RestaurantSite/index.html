Emerson's Eatery was founded by Emerson Miller, himself, back in 2006. Emerson had a dream that he would make the best and most affordable restaurant in town and someday even the country, so he set out to make sure that dream became reality. The Eatery started as a small family run business but almost as if foretold, it quickly outgrew itself as it rapidly gained popularity within the city. Emerson's soon required a specialized crew to handle the daily order influx. From burgers and fries to Italian sausage to house salads, Emerson's has everything you could ask for, so come on in for whatever you're in the mood for! Our friendly staff are excited to help ensure you get your food as ordered and timely manner. Not only do we promise your order to be delicious, but affordable as well, with prices as low as only $6.95! What are you waiting for? Come on down and enjoy the hearty meal you know you deserve.
